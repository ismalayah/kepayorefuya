Beth Britt is an American socialite best known for being the wife of popular professional wrestler and artist, Jeff Hardy.

Beth Britt Biography

Beth Britt was born on April 6, 1978, in the United States. Not much is known about her early life, parents, education, however, she has a brother by the name Phillip Britt.

Beth Britt shot to fame on international media after she began dating Jeff Hardy in 1999. The two met each other in a club in Southern Pines, North Carolina where Jeff and his brother, Matt Hardy went after their first win at the ‘WWF Tag Team Championships’.

Beth Britt and Jeff later tied the knot on the 9th of March 2011 after dating for 12 years. She shares two children with Jeff; their eldest daughter, Ruby Claire Hardy was born on October 19, 2010. They were later blessed with another daughter named Nera Quinn Hardy (born on December 31, 2015).

Beth Britt Height

Beth Britt stands tall at a height of 5 feet 6 inches (1.68 metres).
