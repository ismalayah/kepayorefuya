Wiki/Biography

Vishwak Sen aka Dinneesh Naidu was born on Wednesday, 29 March 1995 (age 27 years; as of 2022) in Dilsukhnagar, Hyderabad, Telangana. His zodiac sign is Aries. He did his schooling at Mother Theresa High School, Telangana. In an interview, he shared how he developed an interest in acting and film direction. He said,

I learnt movie editing when I was in my VII std. The tenants in my house used to run a computer training institute from where I picked up a lot of things, I nevertheless joined an academy that taught me two and half years of multimedia work before the exams. Before wrapping up X std I finished all this. In Intermediate second year I went to an acting school in Mumbai. I was waiting for a good opportunity and nothing was happening. Meanwhile, my friends and I acted in Vellipomake . It was made with a budget of 18 lakhs. After that Ee Nagarainiki Yemaindi and Falaknuma Das happened.”

He then pursued a degree in journalism from Little Flower Junior College, Hyderabad, Telangana. He studied theatre arts form the acting school Actor Prepares, Mumbai.

Career

Controversy

Heated Argument with a News Anchor

On 2 May 2022, a video of Vishwak Sen went viral on social media in which he was asked by the news anchor to get out of the studio. The incident took place when he was invited to the TV9 Telugu news channel for an interview about one of his films. During the interview, the discussion between Vishwak and TV9 Telugu news anchor Devi Nagavalli turned into a heated argument. In the argument, Nagavalli called Sen a ‘pagal sen’ and a ‘depressed person’ for one of his prank videos. Sen then asked her to stop attacking him personally by calling him with such names. Nagavalli shouted at him and asked him to get out of the news studio. As soon as the video went viral on social media, Vishwak’s fans started criticising Devi for her rude behaviour. Whereas, some netizens came in the support of Devi. Later, a case was registered against Vishwak Sen in the State Human Rights Commission for allegedly creating a scene to promote his film.
