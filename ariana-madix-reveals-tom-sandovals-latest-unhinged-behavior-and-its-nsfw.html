Ariana Madix reveals Tom Sandovals latest unhinged behavior and its NSFW Fri 26 Jan

Tom Sandoval is a man known for many things. He’s appearing on Special Forces, returning to Vanderpump Rules, and he’s the owner of a very peculiar flute, too. In a preview of the all-new season, Tom’s ex dishes on what’s topping his priority list…

Being a Bravo star has got to come with a jam-packed schedule. Between performing gigs, filming Vanderpump Rules, competing in other reality TV shows, and reconstructing his life after Scandoval, anyone would think Tom doesn’t have time to think about anything else. But, low and behold, that broken flute of his, he’s found a way to get it back on its shelf, pride of place.

Ariana dishes on her house set-up

During Vanderpump Rules’ new season, Katie Maloney catches up with her pal, Ariana Madix.

Following Scandoval, Ariana, and Tom opted to remain living under the same roof, but avoid each other at all costs. Speaking to Katie, Ariana explains Tom is away shooting a show in New Zealand, hence her “quiet” house.

Ariana explains: “Three months ago, when Tom blew up our lives, I was not prepared to be kicked out of my house and start a whole new life. So, even though we still live under the same roof, I’ve been able to maintain a no-contact policy.”

Ariana ‘feels sorry’ for Ann

Bravo star Ariana updates viewers about how she and Tom are navigating their lives after he ended up having a relationship with Raquel Levis.

She explains they communicate through his assistant, Ann, which is “good” for her, but “bad” for Ann.

Ariana adds: “I don’t have to look at his stupid face, but that’s literally her job.”

Ann is enlisted to be the couple’s go-between for communication, as well as some more bizarre requests from Tom.

Vanderpump Rules flute

As Tom and Ariana move on with their lives after the cheating scandal, 40-year-old Tom apparently “isn’t bothered” by his ex having a new partner.

One thing he is concerned about, however, is an ornamental flute shaped like a man’s bits.

Speaking to Katie on Vanderpump Rules, Ariana tells “the latest of Tom’s unhinged behaviors.”

The 38-year-old explains her ex had his assistant “glue the penis flute back together,” as the show cuts to scenes of how the instrument got broken three months prior.

Ariana adds: “He really wants to hold onto that thing.”
