WWE Announces Gasoline By The Weeknd As WrestleMania 40 Theme Song

The Weeknd continues to be a big part of WrestleMania.

In a tweet, WWE announced that The Weeknd will have an official WrestleMania theme song for the fifth year in a row. In 2024, “Gasoline” will be an official theme song of WrestleMania 40.

Blinding Lights” was the theme song of WrestleMania 36, while The Weeknd went back-to-back with “Save Your Tears” for WrestleMania 37. WrestleMania 38 had “Sacrifice” by The Weeknd & Swedish House Mafia, while WrestleMania 39 had “Less Than Zero” by The Weeknd.
